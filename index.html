Field Fusion

2024

Unified computer vision platform for comprehensive football match analysis supporting real-time multi-object tracking, team classification, ball possession analysis, and player performance metrics across 4K video at 25 FPS.

System Architecture

  • Multi-Object Detection: YOLOv8 + ByteTrack for simultaneous tracking of players, referees, and ball with consistent ID maintenance
  • Team Classification: K-means clustering on jersey colors with corner pixel analysis for background separation
  • Ball Possession: Distance-based assignment with multi-point calculation and temporal consistency tracking
  • Camera Compensation: Lucas-Kanade optical flow for robust motion estimation and position correction
  • Coordinate Transformation: Perspective transformation converting pixel positions to real-world field measurements
  • Performance Analytics: Real-time speed (km/h) and distance (meters) calculations with frame rate normalization

Technical Challenges Solved

  • Maintained consistent player identities across frames despite occlusions using YOLO + ByteTrack association
  • Distinguished teams with similar jersey colors through K-means clustering on top-half player crops
  • Compensated camera panning effects on position calculations using optical flow feature tracking
  • Optimized performance with batch processing (20 frames/batch) and efficient numpy operations
